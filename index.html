The Story

This one's for us, Sade.

Fourteen nights. Two world-class hotels. One private speedboat. And nothing but sunshine, warm Red Sea water, and good vibes — that's what this trip is all about.

What We're Doing

The Full Itinerary 🗓️

✈️
Saturday · 28 March
Departure Day — London → Hurghada
Meet at London Luton for our 13:10 easyJet flight. Land in Hurghada and transfer to the legendary Kempinski Hotel Soma Bay. First evening: sundowners on the terrace, dinner overlooking the Red Sea. Welcome to paradise. 🥂
⛵
Sunday · 29 March — 1:00 PM
🌟 HIGHLIGHT: Private Speed Boat Adventure
Our private speedboat departs at 1:00 PM. We're going full Red Sea explorer mode — snorkelling the coral reefs, watching for dolphins, hopping between islands. This is the one. Pack your swimwear and your sense of adventure. 🐬🤿
🏖️
30 March – 4 April
Kempinski Lazy Days
Five glorious days to do absolutely nothing — or everything. Morning beach swims, spa treatments, watersports, sunset yoga, and feasting at the Kempinski's acclaimed restaurants. This is 5-star living at its finest. ☀️
🏨
Saturday · 5 April
Hotel Switch — Rixos Premium Magawish
Check out of Kempinski and check into Rixos Premium Magawish — one of Egypt's most celebrated ultra-all-inclusive resorts. Everything included: food, drinks, entertainment, water park, watersports. It's a full-on holiday destination in itself. 🎉
🌊
5 April – 10 April
Rixos Premium — The Final Chapter
Six nights of ultra-all-inclusive bliss. Live shows, beach parties, multiple pools, beach volleyball, aqua aerobics, sunset cocktails — you name it. Rixos is an experience, not just a hotel. Soak it all up. 🌅
✈️
Friday · 11 April
Farewell Hurghada — Flight Home at 19:45
One last morning on Egyptian soil. Breakfast with a view, final beach walk, then transfer to Hurghada airport for our 19:45 easyJet home. We'll land back in London with tans, stories, and memories for life. 🏡
